About me

Welcome to my website! My name is Endy Godinez. I am a 12th grader at Canoga Park High school. Currently, I am a second year mentee in Northrop Grumman's HIP program. I am a member of MESA and the CPHS Robotics team. I have maintained a 4.0 GPA(unweighted) throughout my high school career. My favorite classes include chemistry, physics and environmental science.

I enjoy binge watching tv shows during my free time. The shows I watch vary from forensics to drama. I also enjoy going out for walks with my dog, Floppy. I like solving puzzles when I need a break from school.

In the year to come, my goal is to be accepted in my top choice university. The goal is to go to the University of California-Los Angeles or University of California-Santa Barbara. My main interest is bioengineering and chemical engineering. I would like to study these majors in the future.
